^(?!.*([\w!@#$%^&*()]{1,})\1\1)(?=.*[A-Za-z])(?=.*\d)[\w!@#$%^&*()]{8,15}$

Username Challenge:
1. Length: between 8 and 15 characters
2. Accepted characters:
   - Numbers (0-9)
   - Alphabetic characters (a-z or A-Z)
   - Special Characters [!, @, #, $, %, ^, &, *, (, )]
3. Must comprise a combination of alphabetic characters and numbers
4. Not contain 3 or more consecutive identical characters
